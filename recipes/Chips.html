Chips

An image of Chips

Chips are crispy, savory snacks loved by many.
Made from sliced potatoes or other ingredients, they are deep-fried or baked to perfection.
Whether enjoyed plain or seasoned with various flavors like salt, barbecue,
or cheese, chips provide a satisfying crunch that satisfies cravings and adds a delightful element to snacking moments.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Select and wash the potatoes.
  2. Slice the potatoes
  3. Soak the potato slices
  4. Dry the potato slices
  5. Heat the oil
  6. Fry the chips
  7. Remove and drain
  8. Season the chips
  9. Repeat and cool
